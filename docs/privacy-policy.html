Privacy Policy

TrailBoss — Outdoor Activity Planner

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

TrailBoss ("the App") is developed by StackStats Apps LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use TrailBoss.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. TrailBoss is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Trip data (trip names, locations, dates, notes, participants) — stored locally on your device only
  • Gear checklists — stored locally on your device only
  • Activity logs — stored locally on your device only
  • Emergency contacts — stored locally on your device only
  • Settings and preferences — stored locally on your device only

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Your data stays on your device and in your iCloud account (if you have iCloud enabled for the app).

Subscriptions

TrailBoss offers optional Pro subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not process payments directly. Subscription management and billing are handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles payment data.

Third-Party Services

TrailBoss does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

Weather Data

Weather features in TrailBoss may use Apple's WeatherKit or similar services to display weather information. Location data used for weather queries is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

TrailBoss does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and does not contain objectionable content.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's Settings > Manage Data section. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app, please contact us:
