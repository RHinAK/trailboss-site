Getting Started

Creating Your First Trip

  1. Open TrailBoss and tap the Trips tab
  2. Tap the + button in the top right
  3. Choose your activity type (Hiking, Fishing, Hunting, Snowmachining, Camping, or Other)
  4. Fill in your trip name, location, dates, and any notes
  5. Toggle "Add Default Gear Checklist" to auto-populate your gear list
  6. Tap Create â€” you're ready to start packing!

Using Gear Checklists

  1. Go to the Gear tab to see all your active trip checklists
  2. Tap a trip to see its gear list
  3. Tap the circle next to each item to mark it as packed
  4. Add custom items using the text field at the bottom
  5. Save your customized list as a template for future trips

Logging Activities

  1. Go to the Log tab
  2. Tap + to create a new log entry
  3. Select the activity type, date, and location
  4. Add notes about conditions, distance, catches, or harvest
  5. Use the filter chips to view logs by activity type

Frequently Asked Questions

How many trips can I create for free?

Free accounts can save up to 3 trips. Upgrade to TrailBoss Pro for unlimited trip planning.

What's included in TrailBoss Pro?

Pro includes unlimited trips, custom gear list templates, enhanced weather integration, detailed activity history, offline access, and trip sharing. Plans start at $5.99/month or $49.99/year, both with a 7-day free trial.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have servers that store your personal information. If you have iCloud enabled, your data may sync across your Apple devices.

Can I delete all my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Manage Data > Delete All Data. This will permanently remove all trips, logs, contacts, and templates.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any active subscriptions associated with your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > TrailBoss Pro to cancel or modify your subscription.

Is the weather data real?

The current version shows weather forecasts to give you an idea of conditions. Full weather integration with real-time data is coming in a future update.

What is a PLB?

A Personal Locator Beacon (PLB) is a device that sends a distress signal to search and rescue services via satellite. Essential for backcountry travel in Alaska and other remote areas where cell service is unavailable. Register yours at beaconregistration.noaa.gov.

Troubleshooting

App is running slowly

Try closing and reopening the app. If you have a large number of trips or logs, consider archiving completed trips.

Subscription not recognized

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID that was used to purchase the subscription.

Data appears missing

Ensure you haven't accidentally deleted data through Settings > Manage Data. Unfortunately, deleted data cannot be recovered.
